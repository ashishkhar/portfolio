Challenges & Solutions

Engineering innovations that overcame complex technical hurdles in threat intelligence visualization.

Entity Extraction Accuracy

Challenge: Accurately identifying cybersecurity entities from unstructured text with domain-specific terminology

Solution: Fine-tuned BERT-based models on cybersecurity corpora with custom entity types and context-aware classification

Graph Scalability

Challenge: Maintaining interactive performance with large threat intelligence graphs containing thousands of nodes

Solution: Implemented graph partitioning, level-of-detail rendering, and WebGL-based visualization to handle complex graphs

STIX Complexity

Challenge: Managing the complexity of STIX 2.1 data model with numerous object types and relationship semantics

Solution: Developed a simplified abstraction layer with intelligent defaults while preserving full STIX 2.1 compliance
